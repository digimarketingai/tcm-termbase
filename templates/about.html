About TCM Termbase

Our Mission

TCM Termbase is a professional bilingual terminology database designed for academic researchers, professional translators, TCM practitioners, and students. Our goal is to provide accurate, standardized translations between Chinese and English for Traditional Chinese Medicine terminology.
